Time-Series Forecasting

2025 • Tools: HTML, CSS, MathJax

Consider an AR(1) model: $$ x_t = \phi x_{t-1} + \epsilon_t, \quad \epsilon_t \sim \mathcal{N}(0,\sigma^2). $$ One-step-ahead forecast is $\hat{x}_{t|t-1} = \phi x_{t-1}$.

Evaluation

Mean Squared Error (MSE): $$ \mathrm{MSE} = \frac{1}{N} \sum_{t=1}^N (x_t - \hat{x}_t)^2. $$ Mean Absolute Error (MAE): $$ \mathrm{MAE} = \frac{1}{N} \sum_{t=1}^N |x_t - \hat{x}_t|. $$
